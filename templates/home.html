Welcome to the Direct Flight Builder!


Click the load button to preprocess flight data from 1990 to 2018. This would take about 90 seconds to load. Afterwards, the Direct Flights calculator will be displayed, a tool that can determine the number of airport pairs which offer direct flights between any two given locations.
